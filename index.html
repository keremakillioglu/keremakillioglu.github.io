About Me

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my blog. I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I am working under the supervision of Prof. M. Tamer Özsu as a member of the Data Systems Group.

Experience

Previously, I did my Master's in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. My Master’s thesis was on query optimization for streaming graph data management systems. I also had research internships on graph databases at Nanyang Technical University, Singapore and the University of Waterloo, Canada. I worked on graph traversal algorithms and analysis of graph query languages. I had an industrial research internship at the Bekaert R&D Centre, China where I worked on a novel research project to utilize workforce in the company. Before starting my Master’s, I worked at Admost as a software engineer. I developed API and server-side services for a diverse customer base.
