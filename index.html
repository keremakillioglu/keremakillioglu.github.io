About Me

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my blog. I am Master’s student in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I am working under the supervision Prof. M. Tamer Özsu as a member of the Data Systems Group.

Experience

I developed API and server-side services for Admost's diverse client base and work closely with Slashpay.
