About Me

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my blog. I am Master’s student in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I am working under the supervision Prof. M. Tamer Özsu as a member of the Data Systems Group. My research focus is query optimization of streaming graph structured data.

Experience

Previously, I had research internships about graph databases at the Nanyang Technical University, Singapore and the University of Waterloo, Canada. I worked on graph traversal algorithms and analysis of graph query languages. I also had the opportunity to have an internship at Bekaert R&D Center, China where I worked on a novel research project to utilize workforce in the company. Before starting my Master’s, I worked at Admost as a software engineer. I developed API and server-side services for a diverse client base.
